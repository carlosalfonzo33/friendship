About Us

How it all began

Friendship gatherings first started at Lounge 3411 in Oakland around 2011. It was primarily a small gathering of friends that would come hang out and listen to good music while catching up with people who hadn't seen each other for awhile. As time when on, the parties became more than just a small gathering of friends. We began to bring out DJ friends from Chicago, Los Angeles, Seattle, and Denver. We kept doing these off and on randomly, and it wasn't until the end of 2016 where we decided to host the friendship parties every three months continuously.

Fast forward to date, 2017 has been fun in bringing out DJ's such as Worthy and Fancy Fux of Dirty Bird Records, Ghettoblaster and DJ Deeon from Chicago, 6blocc from Los Angeles, and Antiserum from San Francisco. Since we've seen a growth in attendance, we've decided to continue hosting them in 2018! We hope you've enjoyed attending these friendship parties as much as we like planning and organizing them for you! A big thank you and much love to all our DJ friends locally who've supported us, it truly means the world and I cannot put into words how much I appreciate every one of you!!
